The only App you need for Lost & Found

Our App is the first Lost & Found system that automates the Lost & Found process while saving you resources as effectively as up to 90 %.

Our mission is to improve your customer’s journey and dramatically increase your customer satisfaction rating — all while actually reducing the overall workload for your Lost & Found team!

Lostfoundapp.com enables your organization to easily implement a fast, reliable and future-oriented system with a clear focus on customer experience and easy usability.

The App makes it incredibly easy for staff to log and manage found items.

All you need to do is take a picture of the found item — that’s it, you’re done! The data is directly loaded in the Lost & Found system thanks to our unique technology. Our image intelligence actually recognizes what the item is, automatically extracts the necessary data and loads it into the correct category with all important attributes like color, model, even brand and serial number already in place.

All with a single picture — it’s just that easy!

Meanwhile, your customer submits an online inquiry by going to the Lost & Found section of your website — a service we will happily provide for you.

Behind the scenes, our Smart Matching Intelligence does its magic and automatically identifies the owners of the lost items.

Your customers then have the choice to either pick up their found items personally or to have them delivered through one of our many logistic partners already fully integrated into the Lost Found App. That’s how we make sure the item arrives where it’s needed when it’s needed.

To ensure the entire shipping experience is seamless for you and your customers, we take care of both the payment and logistics process for you.

LostFoundApp.com even incentivizes your company’s Lost & Found excellence by turning your customers’ positive experiences into revenue. That’s right: We pay you for every item returned using our online return system!

So, don’t get left behind with outdated Lost & Found processes while your competitors have already implemented next-level customer service.

The app’s interface is intuitive and makes it possible for virtually anyone to log found items with a photo and additional information, all while being compliant with your organization’s internal process.

With Lost Found App, it has never been easier to successfully increase your lost item return rate
