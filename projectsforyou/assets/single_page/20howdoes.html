How does the rebate system work?

Giving back is important. At LostandFoundSoftware.com, we make sure you, who are in the business of giving back every day, are rewarded for your hard work.

The Handling Gateway is our payment and shipping provider for lost and found items. It offers a simple and standardized interface to all relevant payment and logistics services. Thanks to state-of-the-art processes, you can send found items back on behalf of your customers with ease.

IHow does it work?

All you need to do is return lost property using our online Handling Gateway (shipping and PCI compliant payment platform) and you will not only gain satisfied customers.

For every item returned, LostandFoundSoftware.com rewards you a rebate. The balance is wired to your account either after a set period of time or reaching the agreed amount.

Is there a specific provider for payments and shipping?

You retain FULL CONTROL! Choose which shipping and payment providers you offer your customers. You can even decide to waive all costs or to charge extra for your expenses (packaging material, storage costs, etc.).

So it’s possible for a company to charge service fees?

The Lost and Found Software lets you (optionally) charge compensation for packaging, handling, storage, and other services you provide. From experience, customers gladly accept reasonable fees for safely finding, storing, and returning their personal property.

Why the Handling Gateway?

Aside from seamlessly automating the return process, additional advantages of the Handling Gateway include being connected to over 10 of the world’s top shipping providers, access to all conventional payment methods (credit card, PayPal, bank wire, etc.), and customers always staying fully informed on the status of their property as well as their ability to choose their preferred payment and shipping methods.

How high is the reward?

Though it depends on the volume of found items, our standard rewards start at 2 USD per returned item.

Does the reward system cost extra?

The Handling Gateway necessary to participate in the reward system is fully integrated into the Lost and Found Software and does not cost you a penny.
