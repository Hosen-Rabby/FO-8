The Do’s and Don’ts for using Image Recognition

Bonus material: Get the best practices guide for Lost and Found Image Recognition and test your knowledge in our quiz.

The LostandFoundSoftware.com image recognition automatically identifies the type of found item, color and location where the item was found. Even details such as brand, serial numbers or info on IDs can be recognized.

The implemented image recognition technology uses the most recent “deep learning” technologies for accurately characterizing images. Information is verified and additional information can be added by users to create a fast and comprehensive found item entry. These items are then immediately and automatically matched with lost items as specified by customers.

The Do’s and Don’ts for using the unique Lost and Found Image Recognition Technology

  1. Do: Image Recognition for capturing data faster

    Use our powerful Image Recognition Technology to accelerate and simplify the registration process for found items. The intelligent system remembers corrections (e.g. “skateboard” instead of “snowboard”) for future picture uploads. Recognizable details include the type of item, brand, serial numbers, and more. Even info on IDs can be recognized via text recognition.

  2. Do: Let Text Recognition do the reading for you

    As just mentioned above, our new Smart Text Recognition uses Machine Learning to scan and extract text from images, adding them to the Software instantly and automatically. The scope includes IMEI numbers, brand names, serial numbers, etc.
