Losing something is frustrating, but the search for what was lost is also a hassle. Not only for the customer, also for all the companies they contact when searching for their missing valuable.

Your company is using an already outdated Lost and Found software provider. But why settle for anything but the best? Insular systems, text search, contact forms — these are all part of yesterday’s prehistoric solutions.

Let’s take a look at the most innovative Lost and Found solution available.

Your customers are used to modern standards. Let’s give them what they expect and deserve!

The perfect partner for your company

A Lost and Found Management Software helps you to concentrate on your core business. We reduce your workload and cost to manage and return items. How? By automating many associated processes (administration, matching lost and found items, return logistics, payment, and more).

Lost and Found Software simplifies managing lost items and gives you MONEY back for every item returned to your customers!

Automatic Matching

The Lost and Found Software automatically matches found items with your guests’ digital lost item inquiries, meaning less work for your staff.

Mobile optimization

Lost and Found Software is also optimized for mobile use and can be more easily used via smartphone and tablet.

All shipment providers

Fedex, UPS, DHL, and more give you and your guests the freedom of choice.

Automatic Speech Recognition

The Lost and Found Software enables your customers to file a lost item report through a simple voice call, all thanks to our automated speech-to-text hotline. This new inquiry option gives your customers the opportunity to provide valuable information through a dedicated phone number connected to the Lost and Found Software. Their messages to your company help recover items faster by allowing callers to use their natural language for their lost property report. Matches are created faster and customers retrieve their belongings sooner!

Paperless

Efficiency, sustainability, usability, standardization — there are many reasons for your Lost and Found Office to go paperless. We provide the tools to make it as easy as possible! With the Lost and Found Software, you can easily fulfill your lost and found requirements. For example, organizing your receipts and signed documents has never been easier! By digitally storing your documents, you’ll reduce the clutter around your workplace as well as your use of paper. Save a tree, save the planet, do an outstanding job!

Works seamlessly on any device

Desktop, mobile, or tablet. The Lost and Found Software was developed in cooperation with and with constant input from industry leaders from public transportation and aviation. Our solutions were built to automate the Lost and Found process for organizations operating on a major national or global scale with millions of passengers. In all of our products, we keep both the companies’ as well as the passengers’ maximum satisfaction in mind. Unlike other software providers, we have made Lost and Found / Lost Property its core business, fully specializing in the service of making the process as effective as possible.

Self-service, open database

You can, if desired, put your found items on a designated landing page and your guests can check for themselves — if the item’s not there, it’s easy to submit a digital lost item inquiry. No unwanted details are published, you have full control of all information!
